Hi team,

A quick reminder on the customer returns process as we head into Q1 peak.

When a customer requests a return, log it in the Returns Module within the same
business day. Returns under $500 can be approved by any team lead. Returns
between $500 and $2,000 need Regional Manager approval. Anything above $2,000
must go to the Finance Controller.

Refunds are only issued after the returned item is received and inspected at
the warehouse. Inspection must be completed within 3 business days of receipt.
Do not promise refund timelines to customers beyond "7-10 business days after
we receive the item."

Damaged-on-arrival items follow a separate fast-track: photo evidence plus a
team lead sign-off is enough to trigger an immediate replacement.
