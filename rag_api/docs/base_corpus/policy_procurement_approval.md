# POL-007: Procurement Request Approval Workflow

**Document Owner:** Finance & Procurement
**Version:** 2.0
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-15

## Scope
This policy governs the approval workflow for all procurement requests raised
through the Procurement Portal.

## Approval Workflow
Approval routing is determined by the total request value:

1. **Under $1,000:** Auto-approved after the requester's direct manager
   acknowledges the request. No formal budget check required.
2. **$1,000 – $10,000:** Requires direct manager approval followed by
   Department Head approval. A budget availability check is mandatory.
3. **$10,000 – $50,000:** Requires Department Head approval and Finance
   Controller approval. Two competing quotes must be attached.
4. **Above $50,000:** Requires Finance Controller, CFO, and a Procurement
   Committee review. Three competing quotes and a business justification memo
   are mandatory.

## Vendor Requirements
All vendors must be on the Approved Vendor List before a purchase order is
issued. Onboarding a new vendor requires a completed vendor due-diligence form
and Finance approval, which typically takes 5 business days.

## Emergency Procurement
Emergency procurement (operational disruption risk) may bypass competing-quote
requirements with written CFO approval, but all other approval tiers still
apply.
